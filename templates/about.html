Our Story

Born in Hawaii, built for Hawaii homes.

SPANKKS Construction started with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable handyman services to Hawaii families. We understand the unique challenges of island living – from salt air corrosion to limited material availability – and we've built our expertise around these realities.

What sets us apart isn't just our technical skills, but our commitment to education. Through SPANKKS SKOOL, we empower homeowners with the knowledge to handle basic repairs safely, while knowing when to call the professionals.

Every project we take on is approached with the same care we'd give our own homes. We're not just building repairs – we're building relationships that last.
