Our Hawaii Story

From garage startup to trusted neighbors.

SPANKKS Construction started as just a husband and wife team working out of our garage. We began doing weekend projects for friends and family – fixing leaky faucets, patching drywall, building small fences. Word spread, and soon neighbors were calling us for help with their homes too.

Living in Hawaii, we quickly learned the unique challenges of island construction – salt air that eats through materials, limited shipping options, and the high cost of everything. But we also discovered the island way of doing business: personal relationships, honest work, and taking care of your community.

Today, we're still just that same husband and wife team. We still answer our own phone, show up when we say we will, and treat every home like it's our own. The only thing that's changed is our experience and the trust our community has placed in us.
