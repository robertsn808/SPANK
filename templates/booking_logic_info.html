Automatic Conversion Rules
When Is Portal Access Granted?
  1. Service Booking: Any service except consultation automatically grants portal access
  2. Status Change: When appointment status becomes "confirmed" or "scheduled"
  3. Manual Activation: Admin clicks "Activate Project" button
Service Booking & Portal Logic
  • Consultations Only: Remain in inquiry stage (no portal)
  • Service Bookings: Automatic portal access granted
  • Drywall, Flooring, etc.: Immediate portal activation
  • Returning Customers: Existing portal access maintained
